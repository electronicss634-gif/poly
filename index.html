About Our Institution

Government Polytechnic College, Perinthalmanna is a premier technical institution established in Kerala, dedicated to academic excellence and student development. We focus on providing quality technical education that equips our students with the skills required to excel in the modern industrial landscape. Our holistic approach ensures that students not only gain technical prowess but also develop leadership qualities and strong moral values.
